Our services

Our services and treatments can be found below.

  • View our prices

Full Groom

Your pooch will be bathed and dried and then fully styled by our expert groomers.

  • Bath and dried
  • Fully styled
  • Pawdicure
  • Ear cleaning
  • Spritz of purfume

Maintenance Groom

This is for when your dog is not quite ready for a full groom. A maintenance groom includes:

  • Bath and dried
  • Tidy paw pads and face
  • Pawdicure
  • Spritz of purfume

Puppy Introduction

For puppy's first groom, bring them to the salon to get them used to the sights, sounds and smells. This is a great way to get them used to being groomed from a young age.

We recommend bringing your puppy into the salon any time after their second vaccinations.

Other Services

Additionally to our above services, we can offer nail trimming as a stand alone service or ear plucking can be added to a full or maintenance groom at no extra charge
