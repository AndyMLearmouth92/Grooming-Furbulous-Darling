Our Services and Prices

Our Grooming Furbulous services and treatments can be found below. If you would like more information, please call us on 07815 592974 or use the contact page and we will be happy to help you.

Please note, we ask that 48 hours be given to change or cancel your appointment. This allows us enough time to offer the appointment to another customer. If 48 hours is not given, unfortunately we must take full payment for the service to cover our costs.

Full Groom

Your pooch will be bathed and dried and then fully styled by our expert groomers.

  • Bath and dry
  • Full style
  • Pawdicure
  • Ear cleaning
  • Spritz of purfume
  • Hygiene clip
  • View our full groom prices

Maintenance Groom

This is for when your dog is not quite ready for a full groom. A maintenance groom includes:

  • Bath and dry
  • Tidy paw pads and face
  • Pawdicure
  • Spritz of purfume
  • Hygiene clip

Maintenance grooms of all breeds are from £22.

Puppy Introduction

For puppy's first groom, bring them to the salon to get them used to the sights, sounds and smells. This is a great way to get them used to being groomed from a young age. A puppy introduction includes:

  • Bath and dry
  • Tidy paw pads and face
  • Pawdicure
  • Spritz of purfume
  • Hygiene clip
We recommend bringing your puppy into the salon any time after their second vaccinations.

Puppy introductions are from £22.

Other Services

Additionally to our above services, we can offer nail trimming as a stand alone service for £8.

Ear plucking can be added to a full or maintenance groom at no extra cost.
